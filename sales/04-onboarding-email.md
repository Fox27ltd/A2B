**Subject:** Welcome to Fox27 — what I need from you to get A2B live this week

---

**2. What I'll do this week:**

- Build your version of the site with your logo + photos in place

- Push it live, give you the keys, confirm everything works end-to-end

**3. Paperwork:**

I've attached the agreement. Have a look, sign and send it back when you're happy. The deposit invoice will follow — happy with bank transfer, card, or cash, your call.

That's it. Reply when you've got 10 minutes, no rush. Any questions, just text me on [your phone].

Cheers,
Callum
